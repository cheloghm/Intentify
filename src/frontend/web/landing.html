The problem

Your website is your best salesperson.
It's also completely silent.

Most teams have no idea who is visiting, what they want, or when they are ready to buy. By the time sales follows up, intent has evaporated.

94%
of visitors leave without identifying themselves

Anonymous sessions account for nearly all your traffic. Without identification, every visit is a missed opportunity you never knew existed.

3+ days
Sales teams follow up days later — when intent is gone

By the time a lead enters your CRM and a rep picks it up, the visitor has moved on to your competitor who engaged them first.

0
Generic chatbots qualify leads. They answer questions.

Most chat widgets are FAQ machines. They create responses, not pipeline. They cannot identify visitors, score intent, or route hot leads to the right rep.

The solution

Hven changes the conversation.

Three capabilities working together from the moment a visitor lands on your site.

Visitor Intelligence

Identify companies and individuals visiting your site in real time. See their industry, company size, pages viewed, and intent signals — before they fill out any form.

AI Engagement

Deploy a trained AI bot that knows your product, qualifies visitors, books demos, and escalates hot leads to your team instantly — grounded entirely in your own content.

Lead Automation

Automatically score, enrich, and route leads into your CRM. Stop chasing cold contacts — focus entirely on visitors who are ready to buy right now.
