How it works

From install to insight in four steps

Add one script tag to your site and Intentify starts working immediately.

Step 01

Install the tracker

Paste a single line of JavaScript onto your website — no developer required. Works with any CMS, SPA, or static site.

Step 02

Identify visitors

Intentify instantly starts building anonymous visitor profiles — page visits, device type, geography, and session patterns — all without cookies.

Step 03

Overlay intent signals

Real-time search intent data from Google is layered onto each visitor, showing you exactly what topics and keywords they're actively researching.

Step 04

Act on the intelligence

Your AI chat widget engages high-intent visitors. Automation flows fire personalised outreach. Ad audiences update automatically. Sales teams get warm leads.

About Intentify

Built for the modern B2B revenue team

Intentify was built because we were frustrated watching 97% of website visitors leave without a trace. The tools that existed were either too expensive, too complex, or too focused on one piece of the puzzle.

We combined visitor identification (what Retention.com does) with real-time search intent intelligence (what Captify does) into one affordable, fast-to-implement platform — designed specifically for lean B2B teams.

Privacy-first by design. GDPR compliant out of the box. No cookies required for core functionality.
